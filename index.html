/ ACT I

The room did not break me. It made an echo chamber.

I wrote my way out before I knew I was escaping.

VickStiX began as survival scribbles, private lightning, and late-night emotional excavation. The songs came first as a place to put what had nowhere safe to land.

Then the vault opened. One song became many. The private became public. The wound grew drums, synths, hooks, teeth, humor, and a little neon venom.

This is the archive of that transformation: the heartbreak, the rage, the weird laughs, the cats, the family ghosts, the political static, the dance floor, the comeback, the fire.
